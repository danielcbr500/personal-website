About Me

Hi! I am Daniel Ahmed and this is my personal website that I created over a weekend of boredom in the summer. I decided to try learning html for fun and as a result I decided to create my own website which also serves as a tool for future employers to get a glimpse of who I am. This website is constantly being updated as I continue to grow my skills.

Where Am I From? I was born and raised in Saddle Brook, New Jersey. After enrolling into college I moved to Jersey City. My parents immigrated here from Pakistan in the 80's and raised me and my siblings in North Jersey.

Two weeks after graduating from Saddle Brook High School, I left for basic military training in San Antonio, Texas where I began my journey in the United States Air Force. After returning from technical training I was put on active duty orders for an extended period of time. During this time, I managed to get my associate's degree in natural sciences and mathematics from Bergen Community College. After returning to tradional reservists status, I enrolled into Rutgers University School of Arts and Sciences located in New Brunswick, NJ. I am currently majoring in computer science with a minor in mathematics with a projected graduation date of May 2026.

In my free time I also serve as a volunteer firefighter and have gone through multiple courses for firefighting receiving certifications from Bergen County Fire Academy. I have been a volunteer for the past 3 years and enjoy everything involved within the fire service from fighting fires to doing community outreach programs. Besides the fire department I also volunteer for a local boys muslim youth group where I am able to help mentor the youth growing up in America with school, identity, and many different issues confronting them. Some of my favorite pastimes include going to the gym, hiking/exploring nature, and watching the UFC.
